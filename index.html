Financial Formulae (Math)

Net Present Value (NPV):

$$\mathrm{NPV}(r, C_0,\ldots,C_T)=\sum_{t=0}^{T}\frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t}$$

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

$$\mathrm{WACC}=\frac{E}{D+E}R_e+\frac{D}{D+E}R_d(1-T)$$
